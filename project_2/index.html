About Us

Toronto Cupcake is one of Canada's premiere destinations for gourmet cupcakes and chocolate confections. Delivering to a 100km radius around the GTA, we provide over 30 different cupcakes on a daily basis and will customize any of them for your occasion.

We also offer cupcakes for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, baby showers, bridal showers, and other functions.

Our corporate marketing support services include a custom logo and custom theme, thank you campaigns, and other creative marketing plans.

Our cupcakes are made from the finest ingredients and hand-crafted to make what we believe are the tastiest treats around. We love the idea that our treats make people happy every time they bite into one. How much fun is that!
